How to Play Circle Tap! Swipe left

When the game starts, try to tap all the circles on your screen!

After you tapped on the circle, you will have a short period of time to press the next one.

The more circles you press, the faster you have to get or you will lose.

You can see how much time you have to press the next one, on the bottom of the screen.

You now know the basics of the game!
